About US


No matter how you're feeling, a little dog gonna love you and do remember that anything is paw-sible with a dog by your side!
Whoever had a pet once knows how strong the love between you and your pet can be. A cat, a dog, a hamster. It doesn't matter the animal. Sooner or later you bond with it and can't imagine the world without your furry friend. Every pet I had thought me a lesson.
BlissForBeings connects pet owners with trusted local people who would love to look after their pet. We make it easy to arrange walks, playtime, overnight stays or holidays. Our aim is to help pet owners when they need it, give little furry friend more exercise and playtime and to allow people without a their to furry toddler to spend quality time with one. We call it a win-win (or a woof-woof) situation for everyone involved.
